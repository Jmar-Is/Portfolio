Hello, my name is Jesus Mariscal

I rise above hardship and transform every challenge into an opportunity for excellence.

A Bit About My Journey

I grew up facing real-life challenges that taught me resilience and grit. Coming from a humble background as an immigrant from Mexico, I learned early the value of hard work and determination. I earned my Bachelor's in Biological Sciences from UCI—making the Dean's List for five consecutive quarters—and gained valuable laboratory experience that led to excellence in research and even a publication. Beyond the lab, I scribed for a urologist and volunteered at UCI Medical Center. I was honored as Scribe of the Month in March 2020 and then became a Student Scribes Manager, coordinating activities for the Inland Empire and Orange County. My journey further evolved when I served as a superintendent under a general contracting company for AltaMed—overseeing the renovation and uplift of clinics in South LA, Anaheim, El Monte, and other communities. Today, as an Academic Director, I’m proud to lead, mentor, and create opportunities that empower the next generation.

Why Medicine

For me, true healing transcends the physical—it is a harmonious integration of body, mind, and spirit. Embracing the role of a medic for both the soul and body is not just a career, but a way of life. I believe that every challenge has prepared me to approach healing holistically, and my journey has been about transforming adversity into opportunities for compassionate care.
