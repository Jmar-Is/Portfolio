Overcoming Challenges

My journey has been defined by overcoming adversity. Facing the hardships of a humble upbringing as an immigrant from Mexico taught me the value of perseverance. Every setback became an opportunity to learn and grow, proving that with determination, challenges only make us stronger.

Scribe Recognition and Leadership

Being named Scribe of the Month in March 2020 was a pivotal moment in my journey. That recognition validated my dedication and precision in a demanding clinical environment. Building on that achievement, I assumed the role of Student Scribes Manager—coordinating efforts for the Inland Empire and Orange County—which further refined my ability to lead, mentor, and maintain high standards even under pressure.
