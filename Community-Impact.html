Community Impact

My commitment to community service has shaped my journey—from clinical engagement and volunteer work to leadership in student scribing and healthcare renovations.

Clinical & Volunteer Engagement

As a student scribe for Dr. Kar, I assisted with medical notes and the programming of electronic medical records, managing 7–14 patients a day. I observed in vivo procedures and translated in Spanish to connect with diverse populations. Volunteering at UCI Medical Center further enriched my understanding of community health.

For more details, visit Dr. Kar's Profile.

Student Scribes Leadership

I was honored as Scribe of the Month in March 2020 and then became a Student Scribes Manager, coordinating activities for the Inland Empire and Orange County. This role allowed me to mentor fellow students and streamline clinical documentation.

For more details on the recognition, visit Student Scribes.

Superintendent for Healthcare Renovations

As a superintendent under a general contracting company for AltaMed, I led renovations and improvements across multiple healthcare facilities. Our work included upgrading patient care rooms, reception areas, entire floors dedicated to dental services, and mobile dental units. This role required effective collaboration among electricians, plumbers, carpenters, and other professionals to deliver quality healthcare environments. For further details, visit AltaMed PACE.

Impact in Action

These experiences—from clinical engagement and volunteer work to leadership in student scribing and healthcare renovations—demonstrate my commitment to making a lasting impact on my community through compassion, dedication, and effective collaboration.
